14 Menswear Brands That Celebrate Identity

Latin-infused and sculptural, Gigo’s bodywear is sensual elegance. Known for bold prints in men’s underwear, their pieces bring sophistication to expressive fashion, blending daring visuals with refined tailoring.

A nod to laid-back vibes with sharp edges: Go Softwear offers relaxed fits with editorial polish. Their soft fabrics and wearable silhouettes reinforce that inclusive fashion for bold men can be comfortable and elevated.

Rebellious and raw, Hard Core channels grit and graphic intensity. With cutouts, rugged detailing, and bold visuals, their line is for men who choose fashion that empowers bold choices, pushing boundaries verbally and visually.

13. SUKREW

UK-sophisticated and sensually tuned, SUKREW crafts bold cuts with luxe fabrics. Their designs deliver fashion that empowers bold choices, weaving confidence, edge, and comfort into everyday expression.

Inspired by primal motifs and heritage, ThePack offers tribal prints, bold color, and daring cuts. Their pieces channel underwear that uplifts your confidence, rooted in identity and instinct.
